Read my story

Majid is a passionate Ph.D. Computer Science student at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. Majid is interested in the field of data mining, machine learning, and spatial data mining techniques to address challenges in the intersection of computer science and biology. His current research is to develop a representation learning model to assess the effectiveness of immune check inhibitor (ICI) therapy in cancer treatment for point set data of multiplexed immunofluorescence (MxIF) imagery. Majid is also interested in developing novel spatial data science techniques intersecting in deep learning and high-resolution hyperspectral images of turfgrass in agriculture to evaluate their robustness and performance.

Majid is a detail-oriented student with experience in programming and frontend development in webs and mobile applications. Effective team player with exceptional communication and interpersonal skills. Aiming to use his knowledge of programming and engineering to solve practical problems.
